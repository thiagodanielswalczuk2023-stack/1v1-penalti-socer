# ⚽ 1v1 Penalty Soccer

A fun interactive penalty soccer game built with HTML5 Canvas and JavaScript!

## 🎮 Game Features

- **1v1 Penalty Shootout**: Play against an AI opponent
- **Simple Controls**: Click to aim your shots and defend
- **First to 5 Goals**: Win the match by scoring 5 goals
- **AI Opponent**: Challenging AI with realistic shot patterns
- **Responsive Design**: Play on desktop or mobile
- **Fullscreen Mode**: Play in fullscreen for better experience

## 🕹️ How to Play

### Attacking Phase
1. When it's your turn to attack, the ball appears at your penalty spot
2. Click anywhere on the field to aim your shot
3. The ball will travel toward where you clicked
4. Try to avoid the AI goalkeeper!

### Defending Phase
1. When the AI is shooting, you must defend your goal
2. Click to position your goalkeeper to block the shot
3. The goalkeeper can move up and down to cover different angles

### Winning
- First player to score 5 goals wins!
- Click "Reset Score" to start a new match
