## 🕹️ How to Play

### Basic Controls
```
🖱️  MOUSE MOVE  = Aim your shot
🖱️  CLICK      = Confirm shot
```

### Shooting Process

1. **Choose Direction**
   - Move your mouse to aim
   - Watch the yellow aim reticle
   - Line up your target

2. **Increase Power**
   - The power meter fills automatically
   - Let it reach your desired level
   - Timing matters - too much power misses!

3. **Execute Shot**
   - Click to shoot
   - Watch the ball trajectory
   - Hope the goalkeeper doesn't block!

### Defending
- When AI shoots, the goalkeeper moves automatically
- Try to block their shots
- First to 5 goals wins!

## 📈 Game Mechanics

### Power Meter
- **0-30%** - Weak shot, easy to save
- **30-70%** - Balanced shot, good control
- **70-100%** - Powerful shot, high risk of missing
- **Max Power** - Meter maxes out at 100%

### Aim System
- Aim angle affects ball direction
- Curved shots have slight spin
- Corners are harder to reach but more likely to score
- Center is easier but easier to block

### Goalkeeper AI
- Reaction time: 0.3-0.5 seconds
- Random predictions to add challenge
- Dives based on ball trajectory
- Gets better at defending as game progresses
